Playpower Foundation makes fun, affordable and effective learning games on $10 computer platform. It is based in Carnegie Mellon University, USA.

I was a game developer at playpower. My duties were to write assembly code for 6502 processor and make game. I helped in making 3 games namely Hanuman Warrior Typing game, Hanuman Quiz game and Malaria game. These games were tested in Ahmedabad, Mumbai and Bengaluru and received a fabulous response.
