Self Dispensing Jug

In Feb 2009, I and my friends participated in Re-Kriti. It is an event organized during Synapse 2009 and was supported by National Innovation Foundation. The aim of the event was to provide some innovative solution for a given problem. Our team was taken a task to design a Self Dispensing Jug.

The problem statement mentioned that the jug should be able to detect the presence of glass, so we had a LDR circuit to do this function. It also mentioned that the jug should stop dispensing automatically. Therefore we had a timer circuit. We also had a water level detector in tank which detects low water level. Apart from these, we also kept an electromagnetic valve for water ejection.

The whole system could be assembled in just Rs. 350. We won consolation prize for it.
